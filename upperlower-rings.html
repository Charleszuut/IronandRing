8-Week Program Guide

Upper / Lower
Rings

A 4-day ring-based split that alternates upper and lower sessions for balanced growth. It combines ring instability, bodyweight progressions, and band work to build size, control, and resilient joint mechanics.

Days / Week
4
Program Length
8 Weeks
Session Time
~50 Min
Equipment
Rings + Bands
Stretch-Priority Loading
Controlled Eccentric 3-4s
RIR-Based Intensity
Synergist Separation

Iron & Ring Approach

This split keeps push and pull stress organized while lower days build strong hips, hamstrings, and quads with minimal equipment. You will progress by cleaner execution, then harder leverage and load. Control at long muscle lengths drives growth. Stay patient and consistent with tracking.
