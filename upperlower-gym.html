8-Week Program Guide

Upper / Lower
Split

This 4-day structure alternates upper and lower sessions to build size while managing fatigue. Exercise order favors long-length loading, controlled eccentrics, and synergist separation so progress stays sustainable week to week.

Days / Week
4
Program Length
8 Weeks
Session Time
50-60 Min
Equipment
Full Gym
Stretch-Priority Loading
Controlled Eccentric 3-4s
RIR-Based Intensity
Synergist Separation

Iron & Ring Approach

Upper/Lower gives you enough frequency to grow without overlapping fatigue on consecutive days. Stay inside the rep ranges, hit tempo targets, and progress using double progression. Most sets should end with clean reps in reserve, not breakdown reps. The result is consistent progression instead of random burnout.
