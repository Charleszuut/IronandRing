8-Week Program Guide

Push / Pull /
Legs

A 6-day split for lifters who can recover from higher weekly frequency. The structure rotates emphasis across Push, Pull, and Legs while preserving stretch-mediated loading, strict eccentrics, and consistent RIR control.

Days / Week
6
Program Length
8 Weeks
Session Time
50-60 Min
Equipment

Stretch-Priority Loading
Controlled Eccentric 3-4s
RIR-Based Intensity
Double Progression Tracking

Iron & Ring Approach

PPL works when effort is high and execution is tighter than average. You will use low-to-moderate set counts, push quality sets close to target RIR, and avoid overlapping fatigue through session design. Keep tempo honest and progress patiently. Consistency over eight weeks beats random maximal sessions.
